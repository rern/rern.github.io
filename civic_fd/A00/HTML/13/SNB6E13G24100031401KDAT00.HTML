Heater Control Panel/Rear Window Defogger Switch Removal and Installation

1.Remove the center panel.

2.Remove the dials (A), the self-tapping screws, and the heater control panel (B).

 

3.Install the control panel in the reverse order of removal. After installation, operate the various functions to make sure they work properly.

4.Run the self-diagnostic function to confirm that there are no problems in the system.
