Fuel Tank Replacement

1.Drain the fuel tank, then disconnect fuel tank unit 4P connector (A) and quick-connect fittings (B).

 

2.List up the vehicle, and support it with jackstands.

3.Remove the cover (C), and EVAP canister guard (D).

4.Disconnect the hoses (E). Slide back the clamps, then twist the hoses as you pull to avoid damaging them.

5.Place a jack or other support under the tank (F).

6.Remove the strap bolts and the strap (G).

7.Remove the fuel tank.

8.Install the parts in the reverse order of removal.
