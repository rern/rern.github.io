A/T Interlock System - Park Pin Switch Test

NOTE: The illustration shows the LHD model; RHD model is similar.

1.Remove the shift lever assembly.

2.Move the shift lever into the P position, and check for continuity between park pin switch/A/T gear position indicator panel light connector terminals No. 3 and No. 4. There should be no continuity.

 

3.Shift out of the P position, and check for continuity between connector terminals No. 3 and No. 4. There should be continuity.

4.If the park pin switch tests OK, install the shift lever assembly.
If the park pin switch fails the test, replace the park pin switch.
