A/T 3rd Clutch Transmission Fluid Pressure Switch Replacement

1.Disconnect the negative cable from the battery, then disconnect the positive cable.

2.Remove the battery hold-down bracket, and remove the battery and battery tray.

3.Remove the air intake duct and air cleaner housing.

4.Remove the battery base and resonator.

5.Disconnect the 3rd clutch transmission fluid pressure switch connector, then remove the 3rd clutch transmission fluid pressure switch (A).

 

6.Make sure there is no water, oil, dust, or foreign particles inside the connector.

7.Install the new 3rd clutch transmission fluid pressure switch with a new sealing washer (B), and tighten the metal part of the switch.

8.Connect the connector securely.

9.Install the battery base and resonator.

10.Install the air cleaner housing and air intake duct.

11.Install the battery tray and battery, then secure the battery with its hold-down bracket.

12.Connect the battery terminals, and apply grease around the battery terminals.

13.Do the power window control unit reset procedure.
