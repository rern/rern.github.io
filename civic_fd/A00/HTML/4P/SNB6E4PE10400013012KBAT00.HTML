ATF Cooler Hose Replacement

1.Slide the ATF cooler/filter hose (A) on the ATF filter (B) until the hose end is 5-7 mm (0.20-0.28 in.) (C) away from the filter housing, and secure the hoses with the clip (D) at 10-12 mm (0.39-0.47 in.) (E) from the filter housing.

 

2.Slide the ATF cooler/filter hose (F) with white dot end (G) on the ATF filter until the hose end is 5-7 mm (0.20-0.28 in.) away from the filter housing, and secure the hose with the clip (H) at 10-12 mm (0.39-0.47 in.) from the filter housing.

3.Install the ATF cooler/filter hose on the clamp (I) at the white mark (J).

4.Slide the ATF cooler/filter hose (K) over the ATF cooler line (L) until the hose end contacts the bulge (M), and secure the hoses with the clip (N) at 2-4 mm (0.08-0.16 in.) (O) from the hose end.

5.Slide the ATF cooler outlet hose (P) with white dot end (Q) over the ATF cooler line (R) until the hose end contacts the bulge (S), and secure the hose with the clip (T) at 2-4 mm (0.08-0.16 in.) (U) from the hose end.

6.Slide the ATF cooler outlet hose (A) with pink dot end (B) over the ATF outlet line (C) until the hose end contacts the bulge (D), and secure the hose with the clip (E) at 2-4 mm (0.08-0.16 in.) (F) from the hose end.

 

7.Slide the ATF cooler inlet hose (G) with pink dot end (H) over the ATF inlet line (I) until the hose end contacts the bulge (J), and secure the hoses with the clip (K) at 6-8 mm (0.24-0.31 in.) (L) from the hose end.
