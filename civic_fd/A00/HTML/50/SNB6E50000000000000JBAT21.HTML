Visually inspect the following items:

  • Check for correct installation and position, check for cracks, deterioration, rust, and leaks
  • Check tightness of screws, nuts, and joints. If necessary, retighten
 
Tie-rod ends, steering gearbox, and boots
  • Check rack grease and steering linkage
  • Check the boot for damage and leaking grease
 		l l l l l l l l l l 
Suspension components
  • Check the bolts for tightness
  • Check all dust cover for deterioration and damage
 
Driveshaft boots
  • Check the boot and boot band for cracks
  • Check rack grease
 
Brake hoses and lines (including ABS)
   Check the master cylinder, proportioning control valve, and ABS    modulator-control unit and leakage 
All fluid levels and condition of fluids
   Check levels and check for leaks. If necessary, add
  • Engine coolant R18A1, R18A3 engines, K20Z2 engine
  • MTF SPFM manual transmission, SPGM manual transmission, RPD6 manual transmission
  • ATF SPCA automatic transmission, MPMA automatic transmission
  • Clutch fluid SPFM, SPGM manual transmission, RPD6 manual transmission
  • PSF
  • Brake fluid
  • Windshield fluid
  • Battry fluid
 
Exhaust system R18A1, R18A3 engines, K20Z2 engine
   Check the exhaust pipe and muffler for damage, leaks, and tightness 
Fuel lines and connections R18A1, R18A3 engines, K20Z2 engine
  • Check fuel lines for loose connections, cracks, and deterioration
  • Retighten loose connections and replace any damaged parts
