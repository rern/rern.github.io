M/T Reverse Lockout Solenoid Test

1.Remove the reverse lockout solenoid.

2.Connect battery positive terminal to the No. 2 terminal of the reverse lockout solenoid 2P connector, and connect the battery negative terminal to the No. 1 terminal.

 

3.Check that the reverse lockout solenoid operates.

4.If the reverse lockout solenoid does not work, replace it.
