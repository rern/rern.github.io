M/T Assembly Installation

1.Loosen the upper torque rod mounting bolt (A).

 

2.Check that the two dowel pins are installed in the clutch housing.

3.Apply urea grease UM264 (P/N 41211-PY5-305) to the release fork (A) and the release bearing (B). Install the release fork and the release bearing.

 

4.Place the transmission on the transmission jack, and raise it to the engine level.

5.Install the lower transmission mounting bolts.

 

 

6.Install the clutch cover.

 

7.Install the intermediate shaft.

 

8.Install the driveshafts inboard joint.

9.Support the subframe with a 4 x 4 x 40 in. Piece of wood and a jack.

 

10.Install the front suspension subframe (A). Loosely install the new subframe mounting bolts (B).

 

11.Align all reference marks on the body (A) and subframe (B). Tighten the subframe mounting bolts to the specified torque.

 

12.Install the lower torque rod mounting bracket.

 

13.Install the lower torque rod mounting bolts.

 

14.Install the middle subframe mounting bolts.

 

15.Install the steering gearbox stiffner plates (A) and mounting bracket (B). Tighten the mounting bolts to the specified torque.

 

 

16.Install the steering line bracket (A) and the clip (B).

 

17.Connect the lower arm.

18.Install the transmission mounting bracket base (A) and stud bolts (B).

 

19.Install the transmission mounting bracket bolts (A). Install the transmission mounting bolt (B) and nuts (C) then install the ground cable (D).

 

20.Loosen and retighten the lower torque rod mounting bolt.

 

21.Install the splash shield.

 

22.Tighten the upper torque rod mounting bolt.

 

23.Install the upper transmission mounting bolts.

 

24.Remove the engine hanger (A) and bolts (B).

 

25.Install the harness holder bracket (C) and air cleaner housing bracket (D), then install the breather pipe (E) and the harness holder (F).

26.Connect the vehicle speed sensor connector (A).

 

27.Apply a light coat of urea grease 264 (P/N 41211- PY5- 305) to the cable ends (A), and the surface of the wire end rubber (B) with silicone grease.

 

28.Install the cable bracket (C) and the cables (D), then install the new cotter pins (E).

29.Connect the back-up light switch connector (A) and the reverse lockout solenoid connector (B), then install the engine harness cover (C) and the harness clips (D).

 

30.Apply urea grease UM264 (P/N 41211-PY5-305) to the end of the cylinder rod. Install the slave cylinder (A), then install the clutch line bracket (B). Be careful not to bend the clutch line.

 

31.Install the battery base (A) with the reservoir tank (B).

 

32.Install the harness clips (C) and the intake air duct (D).

33.Install the air cleaner housing.

34.Install the battery. Connect the positive (+) cable first, then the negative (-) cable to the battery.

35.Install the under-cowl panel and cowl cover.

36.Refill the transmission fluid.

37.Test-drive the vehicle.

38.Check the clutch operation.

39.Check the front wheel alignment.

40.Enter the anti-theft code for the radio and navigation system, then enter the customer's radio station presets.

41.Do the power window control unit reset procedure.
