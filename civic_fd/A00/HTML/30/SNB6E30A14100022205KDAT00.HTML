Drive Belt Auto-tensioner Removal/Installation

1.For models if equipped with power steering fluid cooler: Remove the bolts (A) securing the power steering fluid line brackets, and remove the power steering fluid line from the clamps (B).

LHD model 

RHD model 

2.Loosen the water pump pulley mounting bolts.

 

3.Remove the drive belt.

4.Remove the alternator.

5.Remove the water pump pulley.

 

6.Remove the drive belt auto-tensioner.

 

7.Install the drive belt auto-tensioner in the reverse order of removal.
