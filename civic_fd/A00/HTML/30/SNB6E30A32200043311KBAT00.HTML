Engine Oil Filter Feed Pipe Replacement

1.Remove the filter.

2.Remove the oil pan.

3.Remove the oil filter feed pipe.

 

4.Install two 20 x 1.5 mm nuts (A) onto the new oil filter feed pipe. Hold one nut with a wrench, then use a second wrench to tighten the other nut.

 

5.Tighten the oil filter feed pipe to 49 N·m (5.0 kgf·m, 36 lbf·ft), then remove the nuts from the oil filter feed pipe.

6.Install the oil pan.

7.Install the oil filter.
