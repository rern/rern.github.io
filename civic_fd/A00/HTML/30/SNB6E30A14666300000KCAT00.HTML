Cylinder Head Rocker Arm Assembly Installation

1.If the rocker arm assembly is disassembled, reassemble the rocker arm assembly.

2.Install the oil control orifice (A) with a new O-ring (B), then install the rocker arm assembly (C).

 

3.Install the lost motion assembles (D) and the lost motion holder (E).

4.Tighten each bolts two turns at a time in sequence.

Specified Torque 
6 x 1.0 mm
15 N·m (1.5 kgf·m, 11 lbf·ft) 

 

5.Adjust the valve clearance.

6.Install the cylinder head cover.
