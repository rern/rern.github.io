EGR Pipe Removal/Installation

Removal


1.Remove the bolts (A) and nuts (B).

 

2.Remove the EGR pipe (C).

Installation


1.Install the EGR pipe to the water passage side, tighten the nuts (A) by the hand with a new gasket (B).

 

2.Install the EGR pipe to the TWC side, tighten the bolts (C) by the hand with a new gasket (D). Then tighten the bolts of TWC side.

3.Tighten the nuts of water passage side.
