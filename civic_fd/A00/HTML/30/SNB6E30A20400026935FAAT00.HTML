F-CAN Circuit Troubleshooting

NOTE: Information marked with an asterisk (*) applies to the CANL line.

1.Turn the ignition switch OFF.

2.Jump the SCS line with the HDS.

3.Disconnect ECM/PCM connector A (44P), then disconnect the HDS.

4.Measure resistance between ECM/PCM connector terminals A36 and A37.

 

Is there about 108-132 W?


YES - Go to 40.


NO - Go to 5.

5.Disconnect the gauge control module (tachometer) 36P connector.

6.Disconnect the ABS modulator-control unit 25P connector (or the VSA modulator-control unit 37P connector and the yaw rate/lateral acceleration sensor 4P connector).

7.Disconnect SRS unit connector A (28P).

8.If equipped EPS system, disconnect EPS control unit connector D (28P).

9.Check for continuity between ECM/PCM connector terminals A36 and A37.

 

Is there continuity?


YES - Repair short in the wires between the ECM/PCM connector terminals A36 and A37*.n


NO - Go to 10.

10.Connect ECM/PCM connector terminal A36 to body ground with a jumper wire.

 

11.Check for continuity between gauge control module (tachometer) 36P connector terminal No. 1 and body ground.

 

Is there continuity?


YES -

  • With ABS system: Go to 12.
  • With VSA system: Go to 13.


NO - Repair open in the wire between the ECM/PCM (A36) and the gauge control module (tachometer).n

12.Check for continuity between ABS modulator-control unit 25P connector terminal No. 1 and body ground.

 

Is there continuity?


YES - Go to 14.


NO - Repair open in the wire between the ECM/PCM (A36) and the ABS modulator-control unit.n

13.Check for continuity between VSA modulator-control unit 37P connector terminal No. 15 and body ground.

 

Is there continuity?


YES - Go to 14.


NO - Repair open in the wire between the ECM/PCM (A36) and the VSA modulator-control unit.n


14.Check for continuity between SRS unit connector A (28P) terminal No. 11 and body ground.

 

Is there continuity?


YES -

  • With electrical power steering: Go to 15.
  • With power steering: Go to 16.


NO - Repair open in the wire between the ECM/PCM (A36) and the SRS unit.n

15.Check for continuity between EPS control unit connector D (28P) terminal No. 1 and body ground.

 

Is there continuity?


YES - Go to 16.


NO - Repair open in the wire between the ECM/PCM (A36) and the EPS control unit.n


16.Remove the jumper wire from ECM/PCM connector A (44P).

17.Connect ECM/PCM connector terminal A37 to body ground with a jumper wire.

 

18.Check for continuity between gauge control module (tachometer) 36P connector terminal No. 19 and body ground.

 

Is there continuity?


YES -

  • With ABS system: Go to 19.
  • With VSA system: Go to 20.


NO - Repair open in the wire between the ECM/PCM (A37) and the gauge control module (tachometer).n


19.Check for continuity between ABS modulator-control unit 25P connector terminal No. 17 and body ground.

 

Is there continuity?


YES - Go to 21.


NO - Repair open in the wire between the ECM/PCM (A37) and the ABS modulator-control unit.n

20.Check for continuity between VSA modulator-control unit 37P connector terminal No. 1 and body ground.

 

Is there continuity?


YES - Go to 21.


NO - Repair open in the wire between the ECM/PCM (A37) and the VSA modulator-control unit.n


21.Check for continuity between SRS unit connector A (28P) terminal No. 12 and body ground.

 

Is there continuity?


YES -

  • With electrical power steering: Go to 22.
  • With power steering: Go to 23.


NO - Repair open in the wire between the ECM/PCM (A37) and the SRS unit.n

22.Check for continuity between EPS control unit connector D (28P) terminal No. 15 and body ground.

 

Is there continuity?


YES - Go to 23.


NO - Repair open in the wire between the ECM/PCM (A37) and the EPS control unit.n

23.Reconnect the gauge control module (tachometer) 36P connector.

24.Measure resistance between ECM/PCM connector terminals A36 and A37.

 

Is there about 108-132 W?


YES -

  • With ABS system: Go to 25.
  • With VSA system: Go to 28.


NO - Substitute a known-good gauge control module. If the HDS identify the vehicle, replace the original gauge control module.n


25.Disconnect the gauge control module (tachometer) 36P connector.

26.Reconnect the ABS modulator-control unit 25P connector.

27.Measure resistance between ECM/PCM connector terminals A36 and A37.

 

Is there about 2.34-2.86 KW?


YES - Go to 34.


NO - Substitute a known-good ABS modulator-control unit. If the HDS identify the vehicle, replace the original ABS modulator-control unit.n


28.Disconnect the gauge control module (tachometer) 36P connector.

29.Reconnect the VSA modulator-control unit 37P connector.

30.Measure resistance between ECM/PCM connector terminals A36 and A37.

 

Is there about 2.34-2.86 kW?


YES - Go to 31.


NO - Substitute a known-good VSA modulator-control unit. If the HDS identify the vehicle, replace the original VSA modulator-control unit.n


31.Disconnect the VSA modulator-control unit 37P connector.

32.Reconnect the yaw rate/lateral acceleration sensor 4P connector.

33.Measure resistance between ECM/PCM connector terminals A36 and A37.

 

Is there about 2.34-2.86 kW?


YES - Go to 34.


NO - Substitute a known-good yaw rate/lateral acceleration sensor. If the HDS identify the vehicle, replace the original yaw rate/lateral acceleration sensor.n


34.Disconnect ABS modulator-control unit 25P connector (or the VSA modulator-control unit 37P connector and the yaw rate/lateral acceleration sensor 4P connector).

35.Reconnect SRS unit connector A (28P).

36.Measure resistance between ECM/PCM connector terminals A36 and A37.

 

Is there about 2.34-2.86 kW?


YES -

  • With electrical power steering: Go to 37.
  • With power steering: Update the ECM/PCM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good ECM/PCM, then recheck. If the symptom/indication goes away with a known-good ECM/PCM, replace the original ECM/PCM.n


NO - Substitute a known-good SRS unit. If the HDS identify the vehicle, replace the original SRS unit.n


37.Disconnect SRS unit connector A (28P).

38.Reconnect EPS control unit connector D (28P).

39.Measure resistance between ECM/PCM connector terminals A36 and A37.

 

Is there about 2.34-2.86 kW?


YES - Update the ECM/PCM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good ECM/PCM, then recheck. If the symptom/indication goes away with a known-good ECM/PCM, replace the original ECM/PCM.n


NO - Substitute a known-good EPS control unit. If the HDS identify the vehicle, replace the original EPS control unit.n


40.Disconnect the gauge control module (tachometer) 36P connector.

41.Disconnect the ABS modulator-control unit 25P connector (or the VSA modulator-control unit 37P connector and the yaw rate/lateral acceleration sensor 4P connector).

42.Disconnect SRS unit connector A (28P).

43.If equipped EPS system, disconnect EPS control unit connector D (28P).

44.Check for continuity between ECM/PCM connector terminal A36 and body ground.

 

Is there continuity?


YES - Repair short in the wire between the ECM/PCM connector terminal A36 and the gauge control module (tachometer), the ABS modulator-control unit, the VSA modulator-control unit, the yaw rate/lateral acceleration sensor, the SRS unit, the EPS control unit, or the DLC.n


NO - Go to 45.


45.Check for continuity between ECM/PCM connector terminal A37 and body ground.

 

Is there continuity?


YES - Repair short in the wire between the ECM/PCM connector terminal A37 and the gauge control module (tachometer), the ABS modulator-control unit, the VSA modulator-control unit, the yaw rate/lateral acceleration sensor, the SRS unit, the EPS control unit, or the DLC.n


NO - Go to 46.

46.Reconnect all connectors.

47.Connect the HDS to the DLC.

48.Disconnect the gauge control module (tachometer) 36P connector.

49.Turn the ignition switch ON (II), and read the HDS.

Does the HDS identify the vehicle?


YES - Replace the gauge control module (tachometer).n


NO -

  • With ABS system: Go to 50.
  • With VSA system: Go to 54.


50.Turn the ignition switch OFF.

51.Reconnect the gauge control module (tachometer) 36P connector.

52.Disconnect the ABS modulator-control unit 25P connector.

53.Turn the ignition switch ON (II), and read the HDS.

Does the HDS identify the vehicle?


YES - Replace the ABS modulator-control unit.n


NO - Go to 62.

55.Reconnect the gauge control module (tachometer) 36P connector.

56.Disconnect the VSA modulator-control unit 37P connector.

57.Turn the ignition switch ON (II), and read the HDS.

61.Turn the ignition switch ON (II), and read the HDS.

63.Reconnect the ABS modulator-control unit 25P connector (or the VSA modulator-control unit 37P connector and the yaw rate/lateral acceleration sensor 4P connector).

64.Disconnect SRS unit connector A (28P).

65.Turn the ignition switch ON (II), and read the HDS.
