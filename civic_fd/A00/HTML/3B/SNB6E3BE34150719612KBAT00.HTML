M/T Differential Carrier Bearings Replacement

Special Tool Required
Driver, 40 mm I.D. (  07746-0030100)
 



1.Check the carrier bearings for wear and rough rotation. If they rotate smoothly and their rollers show no signs of wear, the bearings are OK.

2.Remove the carrier bearing (A) with a commercially available bearing puller (B).

 

 

3.Install the new bearings (A) with the special tool and a press. Press on each bearing until it bottoms. There should be no clearance between the bearings and the carrier.

NOTE: Turn the seal (B) part of the bearing toward the outside of the differential, and install it.
