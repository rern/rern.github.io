DTC Troubleshooting: B1240 (N)

DTC B1240 or DTC indicator N:  A Problem in the Mode Control Linkage, Door, or Motor


1.Clear the DTC with the HDS.

2.Turn the ignition switch OFF, and then ON (II).

3.Do the self-diagnostic with the HDS or climate control unit.

4.Check for DTCs.

Is DTC B1240 or N indicated?


YES - Go to 5.


NO - Intermittent failure.n


5.Turn the ignition switch OFF.

6.Test the mode control motor.

Is the mode control motor OK?


YES - Substitute a known-good climate control unit, and recheck. If the symptom/indication goes away, replace the original  climate control unit.n


NO - Replace the mode control motor, or repair the mode control linkage or door.n
