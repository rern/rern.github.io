Torque Converter Housing Selector Control Shaft Oil Seal Replacement

Special Tools Required
Handle driver (  07749-0010000)
 

Oil seal driver attachment (  07947-ZV00100)
 



1.Remove the oil seal from the torque converter housing.

 

2.Install the new oil seal (A) in the torque converter housing in depth (B) of 0.5-1.5 mm (0.02-0.06 in.) below the housing surface with the special tools.
