A/T Differential Carrier Bearing Replacement

Special Tools Required
Driver 40 mm I.D. (  07746-0030100)
 



NOTE: Check the bearing for wear and rough rotation. If the bearing is OK, removal is not necessary.

1.Place a collar (A) on the differential carrier, and remove the carrier bearing (B) with the commercially available bearing puller (C).

 

2.Install the new bearing with the special tool and a press. Press the bearing on until it bottoms.
