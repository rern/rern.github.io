A/T Housing Reverse Idler Gear Installation

1.Lightly coat the reverse idler gear shaft (A), needle bearing (B), and new O-rings (C) with lithium grease.

 

2.Assemble the new O-rings and needle bearing on the reverse idler gear shaft, then install the reverse idler gear shaft in the reverse idler gear shaft holder (E). Align the D-shaped cut out of the shaft with the D-shaped area of the holder.

3.Install the reverse idler gear in the transmission housing in the direction shown.

 

4.Install the reverse idler gear shaft/holder assembly in the reverse idler gear.

 

5.Secure the holder with the bolts.
