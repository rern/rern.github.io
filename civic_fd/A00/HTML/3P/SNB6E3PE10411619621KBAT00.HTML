A/T Differential Carrier, Final Driven Gear Replacement

1.Remove the final driven gear (A) from the differential carrier (B). The final driven gear bolts have left-hand threads.

 

2.Install the final driven gear on the differential carrier in the direction shown. Tighten the bolts to 101 N·m (10.3 kgf·m, 74.5 lbf·ft) in a crisscross pattern in two or more steps.
