ATF Filter Replacement

1.Remove the air cleaner housing and air intake duct.

2.Disconnect the ATF warmer hose (A) from the ATF filter (B).

 

3.Remove the ATF filter holder (C).

4.Remove the bolt (D) securing the ATF inlet line (E).

5.Remove the joint bolt (F) from the ATF inlet line.

6.Disconnect the ATF filter from the ATF warmer hose (G), and replace it with new one.

7.Slide the ATF warmer hose on the new ATF filter until the hose end contacts the filter housing, and secure the hose with the clip (H) at 6-8 mm (0.24-0.31 in.) (I) from the filter housing.

8.Install the ATF filter and ATF inlet line/hose with the joint bolt and new sealing washers (J).

9.Secure the ATF filter with the ATF filter holder and the bolt.

10.Slide the ATF warmer hose on the ATF filter until the hose end contacts the filter housing, and secure the hose with the clip (K) at 6-8 mm (0.24-0.31 in.) (L) from the filter housing.

11.Install the air intake duct and air cleaner housing.
