A/T 1st Clutch Reassembly

1.Soak the clutch discs thoroughly in ATF for a minimum of 30 minutes.

2.Install the waved spring (A) in the 1st clutch drum (B). Starting with the clutch waved-plate, alternately install the waved-plates (C) (6) and discs (D) (6). Install the clutch end-plate (E) with the flat side down on the top disc.

 

3.Install the snap ring with a screwdriver to secure the clutch end-plate.

 

4.Check that the clutch piston moves by applying air pressure into fluid passage.
