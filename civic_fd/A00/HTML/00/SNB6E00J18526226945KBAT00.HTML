Front Fender Enclosure Replacement

1.Remove the front inner fender as needed.

2.From the wheel arch, remove the clips.

 

3.Open the front door. Release the hooks (A) securing the front fender enclosure (B).

 

4.Remove the front fender enclosure (A).

 

5.Install the fender enclosure in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:

  • Check for damaged or stress-whitened clips, and if necessary, replace them with new ones.
  • Push the clips and hooks into place securely.
