High Mount Brake Light Replacement

1.Open the trunk lid.

2.Disconnect the 2P connector (A), then turn the bulb socket (B) 45 ° counterclockwise to remove the bulb.

Bulb type: 21 W 

 

3.Remove the rear shelf.

4.Remove the high mount brake light (A).

 

5.Install the light in the reverse order of removal.
