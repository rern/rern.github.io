Front Fender Trim Replacement

NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the fender trim and body.
  • When removing components, use the handy remover set or equivalent.

1.For some models: Move the steering fluid reservoir (A) as needed.

 

2.Remove the clips.

3.Disconnect the washer mouth (A) from the washer tank (B). Release the hook (C), then remove the front fender trim (D) and the washer mouth.

 

4.Install the fender trim in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:

  • Replace the clips (A) with new ones.
  • Push the clips into place securely.
