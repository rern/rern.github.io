Side Turn Signal Light Replacement

Side Turn


NOTE: Be careful not to damage the fender.

1.Push the retaining spring, and remove the side turn signal light (A).

SIDE TURN SIGNAL LIGHT: 5 W 

 

2.Disconnect the 2P connector (B) from the light.

Mirror Turn


1.Remove the mirror holder.

2.Remove the four screw and viser (A) from the mirror (B).

 

3.Remove the screw, then pull out the side turn signal light (A).

 

4.Disconnect the 2P connector (B) from the light.

5.Install in the reverse order of removal.
