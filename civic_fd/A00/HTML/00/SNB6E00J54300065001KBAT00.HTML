Washer Reservoir Replacement

1.Remove the right inner fender.

2.Disconnect the 2P connectors (A) from the windshield washer motor (B), and headlight washer motor (C).

 

3.Disconnect the windshield washer tubes (D).

4.Remove the bolts (A) and the clip (B), then remove the washer reservoir.

 

5.Install in the reverse order of removal.
