DTC Troubleshooting: 44-2x, 44-3x, 44-Bx

DTC 44-2x (44-20 to 44-29, 44-2A to 44-2F),
44-3x (44-30 to 44-39, 44-3A to 44-3F),
44-Bx (44-B0 to 44-B9, 44-BA to 44- BF):
Internal Failure of the Right Side Impact
Sensor (first)


1.Erase the DTC memory.

2.Turn the ignition switch ON (II), and check that the SRS indicator comes on for about 6 seconds and then goes off.

Does the SRS indicator stay on, and is DTC44-2x, 44-3x, or 44-Bx indicated?


YES - Replace the right side impact sensor (first). If the DTC returns, replace the SRS unit.n


NO - Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Go to Troubleshooting Intermittent Failures. If another DTC is indicated, go to the DTC Troubleshooting Index.
