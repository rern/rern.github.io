Rear Seat Belt Replacement

NOTE: Check the rear seat belts for damage, and replace them if necessary.

Rear Seat Belt Outer


1.Remove the rear seat cushion:

  • Fold down/split fold down
  • Fixed

2.Remove the lower anchor bolt (A).

 

3.Remove these items:

  • Seat side bolsters, fold down/split fold down
  • Seat-back, Fixed

4.Remove the rear pillar trim from both sides:

  • Without side curtain airbags
  • With side curtain airbags

5.Remove the rear shelf.

6.Remove the retractor bolt (A), then remove the rear seat belt (B) and retractor (C).

 

7.Install the seat belt in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:

  • Apply medium strength type liquid thread lock to the anchor bolts before reinstallation.
  • Check that the retractor locking mechanism functions as described.
  • Before installing the anchor bolt, make sure there are no twists or kinks in the seat belt.

Rear Center Seat Belt and Seat Belt Buckles


1.Remove the rear seat cushion:

  • Fold down/split fold down
  • Fixed

2.Remove the center anchor bolts (A), then remove the right seat belt buckle (B), center seat belt buckle (C), and left seat belt buckle (D).

 

3.Remove these items:

  • Seat side bolsters, fold down/split fold down
  • Seat-back, Fixed

4.Remove the rear pillar trim from both sides:

  • Without side curtain airbags
  • With side curtain airbags

5.Remove the rear shelf.

6.Remove the retractor mounting ET screw (A) and the retractor bolt (B), then remove the rear center seat belt (C) and retractor (D).

 

7.Remove the rear center seat belt protector (E).

8.Install the seat belt and buckles in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:

  • Apply medium strength type liquid thread lock to the anchor bolts before reinstallation.
  • Tighten the bolts by hand first, then tighten to specification with a torque wrench.
  • Check that the retractor locking mechanism functions as described.
  • Assemble the washer on the retractor bolt as shown.
  • Before installing the center anchor bolt, make sure there are no twists or kinks in the center belt.
  • Make sure the rear center ELR (emergency locking retractor) is pointing straight forward.
