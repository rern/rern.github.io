Gauge Control Module (Speedo) Trim Removal/Installation

NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the dashboard and related parts.
  • When removing components, use the handy remover set or equivalent.

1.Pry up on the inside edge of the gauge control module (speedo) trim (A) with a trim tool to detach the clips.

 

2.Using a trim tool, detach the clips, and release the hooks (A) and pins (B) along the edge of the gauge control module (speedo) trim (C), then remove the trim.

 

3.Install the trim in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:

  • Check for damaged or stress-whitened clips, and if necessary, replace them with new ones.
  • Push the clips into place securely.
