DTC Troubleshooting: 62-2x

DTC 62-2x (62-20 to 62-29, 62-2A to 62-2F):
Short in Right Side Seat Belt Buckle Switch


1.Erase the DTC memory.

2.Turn the ignition switch ON (II), then buckle and unbuckle the right side seat belt several times.

3.Read the DTC.

Is DTC 62-2x indicated?


YES - Go to 4.


NO - Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Go to Troubleshooting Intermittent Failures. If another DTC is indicated, go to the DTC Troubleshooting Index.


4.Turn the ignition switch OFF.

5.Disconnect the floor wire harness 3P connector from the right side seat belt buckle switch 3P connector (A).

 

6.Connect the HDS to the DLC.

7.Turn the ignition switch ON (II).

8.Make sure the HDS communicates with the vehicle and the SRS unit. If it does not, troubleshoot the DLC circuit:
  • K20Z2 engine
  • Except K20Z2 engine

9.From the Main Menu on the HDS, select SRS, then PARAMETER INFORMATION. In the PARAMETER INFORMATION Menu, select FRONT RIGHT BUCKLE.

Is HDS OPEN indicated on the HDS?


YES - Replace the right side seat belt buckle assembly, then clear the DTC.n


NO - Go to 10.


10.Check resistance between the No. 1 terminal of the floor wire harness 3P connector and body ground. There should be 0-1 W.

 

Is the resistance as specified?


YES - Short to ground in the floor wire harness. Replace the floor wire harness.n


NO - Go to 11.

11.Check resistance between the No. 2 terminal of the floor wire harness 3P connector and body ground. There should be 0-1 W.

 

Is the resistance as specified?


YES - Short to ground in the floor wire harness. Replace the floor wire harness.n


NO - Faulty SRS unit; replace the SRS unit.n
