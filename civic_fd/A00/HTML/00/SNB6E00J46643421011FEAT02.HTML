Door Lock Knob Switch Test - With Super Locking

Driver's Door With Super Locking


1.Remove the driver's door panel.

2.Disconnect the 10P connector from the actuator.

 

3.Check for continuity between the terminals.

  • There should be continuity between the No. 8 and No. 10 terminals when the door lock knob switch is in the LOCK position and no continuity when the switch is in the UNLOCK position.
  • There should be continuity between the No. 9 and No. 10 terminals when the door lock knob switch is UNLOCK position and no continuity when the switch is in the LOCK position.

4.If the continuity is not as specified, replace the door lock actuator.

Front Passenger's Door and Left Rear Door With Super Locking


1.Remove the front passenger's or left rear door panel.

2.Disconnect the 10P connector from the actuator.

NOTE: The illustration shows front passenger's door.

 

3.Check for continuity between the terminals.

  • There should be continuity between the No. 7 and No. 5 terminals when the door lock knob switch is in the UNLOCK position and no continuity when the switch is in the LOCK position.

4.If the continuity is not as specified, replace the door lock actuator.

Right Rear Door With Super Locking


1.Remove the right rear door panel.

2.Disconnect the 10P connector from the actuator.

 

3.Check for continuity between the terminals.

  • There should be continuity between the No. 8 and No. 10 terminals when the door lock knob switch is in the UNLOCK position and no continuity when the switch is in the LOCK position.
