Audio System Symptom Troubleshooting - Audio disc changer does not load all six discs

Audio disc changer does not load all six discs


NOTE: Disc labels should not be used in the audio unit. They may damage the player mechanism.

1.Try loading six discs into the audio unit.

Does the audio unit accept all six discs?


YES - Intermittent failure, the audio unit is OK at this time.n


NO - Go to 2.


2.Try loading the disc player with six known-good discs.

Does the audio unit accept all six discs?


YES - At least one of the original discs is faulty.n


NO - Replace the audio unit.n
