Dashboard Driver's Lower Cover Removal/Installation

NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the dashboard and related parts.
  • When removing components, use the handy remover set or equivalent.

1.Adjust the steering column upward.

2.Gently pull out on the lower edge of the dashboard lower cover (A) to detach the lower clips.

 

3.Detach the upper clips, and release the hook (A) and pin (B) by pulling the driver's dashboard lower cover (C) back.

 

4.Install the lower cover in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:

  • Check for damaged or stress-whitened clips, and if necessary, replace them with new ones.
  • Push the clips into place securely.
