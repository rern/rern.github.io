Dashboard Driver's Under Cover Removal/Installation

NOTE: Take care not to scratch the dashboard and related parts.

1.Remove the driver's dashboard under cover (A).

-1 Turn the lock knob (B) 90 °.
-2 Gently pull down the rear edge to detach the clips.
-3 Pull the cover away to release the pin (C) from the holder (D).

 

2.Install the under cover in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:

  • Check for damaged or stress-whitened clips, and if necessary, replace them with new ones.
  • Push the clips into place securely.
