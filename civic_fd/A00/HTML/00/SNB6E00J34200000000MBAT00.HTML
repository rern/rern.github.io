Resetting the Sunroof Control Unit

Resetting the sunroof is required when any of the following have occurred:

  • The sunroof was moved manually while the battery was dead or disconnected.
  • The sunroof motor was replaced with a new one.
  • Any of components related to the sunroof were replaced.
    • Wind deflector
    • Sunroof glass
    • Sunroof seal
    • Sunroof glass bracket
    • Sunroof cables, etc.

To reset the sunroof control unit, do these steps:

1.Turn the ignition switch OFF.

2.Press and hold the tilt switch, and turn the ignition switch ON (II).

3.Release the tilt switch, and turn the ignition switch OFF.

4.Repeat steps 2 and 3 four times.

5.Press and hold the sunroof open switch for 3 additional seconds after the sunroof is fully opened.

6.Press and hold the sunroof close switch for 3 additional seconds after the sunroof is fully closed (tilted).

7.Confirm that the sunroof control unit is reset by using the moonroof AUTO OPEN and AUTO CLOSE function.
