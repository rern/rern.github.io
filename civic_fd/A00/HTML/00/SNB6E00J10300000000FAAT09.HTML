Audio System Symptom Troubleshooting - Audio system sound is weak or distorted (display is normal)

Audio system sound is weak or distorted (display is normal)


1.Check for sound in each mode (AM, FM, and tape/disc).

Is there sound from the speakers, and is the sound quality normal in each mode?


YES - Intermittent failure. The system is OK at this time. Check for loose connections at the audio unit, amplifier, and each speaker.n


NO - Speakers all work, sound quality is poor.n
If the sound quality is poor only with the AM or FM radio, go to poor radio reception or interference.
