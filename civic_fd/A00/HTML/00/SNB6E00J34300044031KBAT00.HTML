Power Mirror Actuator/Retract Actuator Replacement

1.Remove the mirror holder, and the power mirror.

2.Disconnect the 3P (or 6P)*1 (or 8P)*2 connector from the mirror.

*1: With retractable power mirror or power mirror defogger or mirror turn 
*2: Mirror turn and retractable power mirror or power mirror defogger 


3.With retractable power mirror:
If replace the power mirror actuator, disassemble the power mirror 6P connector (A), and remove the No. 1 and No. 3 terminals or 8P connector No. 1 and No. 2 terminals from it.
If replace the retract actuator, disassemble the power mirror 6P connector (A), and remove the No. 4, No. 5 and No. 6 terminals or 8P connector No. 6, No. 7 and No. 8 terminals from it.

4.Record the terminal locations and wire colors.

5.Disassemble the power mirror 8P connector, and remove all terminals from it, and remove the screw from the gasket (B).

 

6.Remove the screws and the mirror visor (A).

 

7.Remove the three screws, and separate the mirror housing (A) from the bracket (B).

 

8.Remove the four screws, and the actuator (A).

 

9.Disconnect the 2P connector (B) from the side turn signal light.

10.Route the wire harness (A) of the new actuator through the hole in the bracket (B) and gasket (C).

 

11.Install the actuator, bracket, harness clip (D), and gasket in the reverse order of removal.

12.Insert the new actuator terminals into the connector in the original arrangement.

Left: 

Right: 

13.Apply tape to seal the intersection of the wire harness and the gasket.

14.Reassemble in the reverse order of disassembly. Be careful not to break the mirror when reinstalling it to the actuator.

15.Reinstall the mirror assembly on the door.

16.Operate the power mirror to ensure smooth operation.
