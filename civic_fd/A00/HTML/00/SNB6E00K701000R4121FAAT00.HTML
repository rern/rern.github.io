DTC Troubleshooting: 41-21, 42-21, 43-21, 44-21

DTC 41-21:  Right-front Wheel Lock
  
  
DTC 43-21:  Right-rear Wheel Lock
  
  


The DTCs may indicated under these conditions:
  • The vehicle goes into a spin.
  • The ABS continues to operate for a long time.
  • Snow build-up on the wheel sensor.
  • Misadjusted brake switch
  • Contaminated brake fluid

1.Raise the vehicle, and support it with safety stands in the proper locations.

2.Turn the appropriate wheel by hand.

DTC Appropriate Wheel 
41-21 Right-front 
42-21 Left-front 
43-21 Right-rear 
44-21 Left-rear

YES - Repair the brake drag. n


NO - Go to 3.

Is the wheel sensor installation OK?

NO - Reinstall the wheel sensor, and check the mounting position.n
