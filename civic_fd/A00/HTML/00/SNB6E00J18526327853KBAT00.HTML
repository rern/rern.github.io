Fuel Filler Cap Adapter Replacement

NOTE: Take care not to scratch the body.

1.Remove the cap (A) by turning it counterclockwise, and remove the screw.

 

2.Remove the bolts, and lower the fuel filler pipe (A), then remove it from the fuel filler cap adapter (B).

 

3.Turn the fuel filler cap adapter (A) then remove it.

 

4.Install the adapter in the reverse order of removal.
