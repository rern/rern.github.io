Interior Trim Removal/Installation - Rear Pillar Trim - Without Side Curtain Airbags

Rear Pillar Trim


NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to bend or scratch the trim and panels.
  • When removing components, use the handy remover set or equivalent.

1.Remove these items:

  • Rear seat cushion:
    • Fold down/split fold down
    • Fixed
  • Rear seat side bolster, fold down/split fold down
  • Rear seat-back, fixed
  • Rear door opening seal, as needed

2.Fold the seat-back forward.

3.Pull the rear pillar trim (A) back by hand to detach the clips, then remove it.

 

4.Install the trim in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:

  • Check for damaged or stress-whitened clips, and replace them with new ones.
  • Push the clips into place securely.
