Power Mirror Actuator Test

1.Remove the driver's door panel.

2.Disconnect the 3P (or 6P), (or 8P) connector (A) from the power mirror actuator (B).

 

3.Check actuator operation by connecting power and ground according to the table.

 

Retract Test 

4.If the mirror fails to work properly, replace the mirror actuator.

Defogger Test


5.Check for continuity between the No. 2 and No. 1 terminals of the 8P connector. There should be continuity with the defogger switch ON. If there is no continuity, check for an open circuit.
