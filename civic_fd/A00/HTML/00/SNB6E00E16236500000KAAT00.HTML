Intermediate Shaft Removal

Procedure for R16A, R18A engine models


1.Remove the right driveshaft.

2.Remove the lower torque rod bracket (A).

 

3.Remove the flange bolts.

 

4.Remove the intermediate shaft (A) from the differential. Hold the intermediate shaft horizontal until it is clear of the differential to prevent damage to the differential oil seal (B).

 

Procedure for K20Z2 engine model


1.Remove the right driveshaft.

2.Remove the heat cover.

 

3.Remove the flange bolt (A) and two dowel bolts (B).

 

4.Remove the intermediate shaft (A) from the differential. Hold the intermediate shaft horizontal until it is clear of the differential to prevent damage to the differential oil seal (B).
