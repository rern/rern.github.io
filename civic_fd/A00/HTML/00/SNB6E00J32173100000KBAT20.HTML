Inner Taillight Replacement

1.Open the trunk lid, and remove the rear license trim.

2.Disconnect the connectors (A) from the inner taillight (B).

Taillight: 5 W 
Back-up Light: 18 W 
Rear Fog Light: 21 W 

 

3.Turn the bulb socket (B) 45 ° counterclockwise to remove the bulb.

4.Remove the nuts, then remove the taillight.

5.Install the light in the reverse order of removal.
