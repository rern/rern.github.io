Center Console Disassembly/Reassembly

NOTE:
  • When removing components, use the handy remover set or equivalent.
  • Take care not to scratch the center console, dashboard, and related parts.

1.Remove the center console.

2.Remove the center console arm rest.

3.Using a T20 Torx bit, remove the screws.

 

4.Release the hooks (A) and pins (B), then separate the left console side panel (C), right console side panel (D), console beverage holder (E) and console box (F).

 

5.Release the hooks (A, B), then separate the console upper box (C) and console lower box (D).

 

6.Install the console in the reverse order of removal.
