Rear Floor and Rear Floor Cross-member Removal

  • Cut the rear floor 15 mm (0.6 in.) from the welded flange of the rear floor cross-member.
  • Check the rear floor side stiffener position for damage.
  • If necessary, replace the rear floor side stiffener B.

 

Check rear frame B and the rear floor cross-member position for damage. If necessary, replace them.
