Rear Floor and Rear Floor Cross-member Installation

1.Clamp the new rear frame B and rear floor cross-member, and check the body dimensions.

  • Front floor and rear floor under view
  • Repair chart top view
  • Repair chart side view

2.Tack weld rear frame B and the rear floor cross-member into position.

3.Check the rear floor cross-member (A), rear frame A (B) and rear frame B (C) with rear suspension, then weld them into place.

 

 

4.Plug weld the new rear floor cross-member (A), and rear floor (B). Finish the plug welded area.

NOTE: Do not grind the welds excessively.

 

5.Clamp the new rear floor side stiffener B (A) into position, align it with rear frame B (B) and tack weld it.

 

6.Cut the new rear floor (A) so it overlaps the body side floor (B) by about 40 mm (1.6 in.). Set the new rear floor into position, and align it with the body.

 

7.Tack weld the new rear floor, and rear floor side stiffener.

8.Clamp the rear panel, check the position and body dimensions.

Rear window and trunk lid opening.

9.Do the welding.
Weld the rear floor (A) and rear floor side stiffener B (B).

 

10.Weld the rear floor side stiffener B (A) to the rear wheelhouse (B) and outer panel (C).
