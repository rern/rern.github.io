Side Sill Outer Panel Removal

  • Cut and pry off the side sill outer panel, and replace the front pillar outer separator and center pillar outer separator.
  • If there is any damage to the center pillar, cut the outer panel.

 

  • Check the side sill reinforcement, center pillar stiffener position, and check for damage.
  • If necessary, cut the center pillar stiffener, and replace the side sill reinforcement.
  • When removing the center pillar stiffener, replace the center pillar inner separator.
