Rear Panel Installation

1.Set the new rear panel into place, and check the body dimensions.

  • Front floor and rear floor under view
  • Rear window and trunk lid opening.

2.Tack weld the new parts.

3.Temporarily install the trunk lid, taillight, and rear bumper, then check for defferences in level and clearance. Check the operation of the trunk lid.

4.Do the main welding.
Weld the rear panel (A) and rear panel side stiffener (B).
