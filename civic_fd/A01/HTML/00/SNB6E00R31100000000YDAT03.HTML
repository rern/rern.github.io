Front Bulkhead Installation

1.Set the new parts bulkhead and measure the front compartment diagonally.

2.Check the body dimensions.

  • Engine compartment
  • Front damper extension position
  • Engine compartment and front floor under view
  • Repair chart top view
  • Repair chart side view

3.Tack weld the new parts into position.

4.Temporarily install the hood, front fender, headlight, and front bumper, then check for differences in level and clearance.

5.Do the main welding.

  • Weld the front side frame (A) and bulkhead side stay (B).
  • Weld the subframe front bracket (C) and bulkhead side stay.

 

 

6.Remove the bulkhead upper center frame (A).
Weld the bulkhead side stay (B) and bulkhead upper side frame (C).

 

7.Weld the bulkhead upper side frame (A) to the front damper extension (B), and install the bulkhead upper center frame (C).
