Side Sill Outer Panel Installation

1.Set the new side sill reinforcement, and tack weld it into position. Cut the new center pillar stiffener (A) so it overlaps the body side stiffener by about 20 mm (0.8 in.).

 


2.Install the center pillar inner separator (A) to the side sill reinforcement (B).

NOTE: Apply the sealer (C) all the way around the separator inside of the center pillar stiffener (D), without gaps. Clamp the new center pillar stiffener, and tack weld it into position.

 

3.Cut the new outer panel (repair part) to align it with the body.

4.Clamp the new outer panel, and check the body dimensions.

  • Passenger's compartment
  • Engine compartment and front floor under view
  • Front floor and rear floor under view
  • Windshield and door opening

5.Temporarily install the windshield, door, hood, and front fender, and then check for differences in level and clearance. Make sure the body lines flow smoothly.

6.Remove the new outer panel, and weld the side sill reinforcement (A), and center pillar stiffener (B). Weld the front edge of the side sill reinforcement and front pillar lower stiffener.

 

7.Install the center pillar outer separator (A) to the center pillar stiffener (B).

NOTE: Apply the sealer (C) all the way around the front pillar outer separator, center pillar outer separator and inside of the new outer panel (D), without gaps.
Clamp the new outer panel, and recheck the clearance and alignment of the door. Check the operation of the doors.

 

8.Do the main welding.

  • Attach the patch (A) at the cut section (B) of the outer panel (C), and plug weld them.
  • The roof area must be free of burrs and/or sharp edges to prevent damage to the side curtain airbag during deployment.
