Front Side Frame/Outrigger Installation

1.Position the front bulkhead, and measure the front compartment diagonally.

2.Check the body dimensions.

  • Engine compartment
  • Engine/transmission mount positions
  • Front damper extension position
  • Engine compartment and front floor under view
  • Repair chart top view
  • Repair chart side view

3.Tack weld the new parts and front bulkhead into position.

4.Temporarily install the front subframe, and check the front side frame and side frame extension position.

5.Temporarily install the hood, front fender, headlight, and front bumper, then check for differences in level and clearance. Make sure the body lines flow smoothly.

6.Do the main welding.

  • Weld the front side frame (A), side frame extension (B), outrigger (C), subframe rear bracket (D).
  • From the passenger's side, plug weld the holes in the dashboard lower (E) and front floor (F).

 

 

 

 

7.Main weld the front side frame (A) to the damper housing (B) and front wheelhouse (C).

 

8.Weld the front damper extension (A) to the front end outrigger (B).
